Frequently Asked Questions

01. What is the objective of our portal?

The objective of this portal is to provide a common platform for all bidders to go through the tenders and apply accordingly and a single point access to the procurements made across various central government organisations.

02. What all would be available?

It contains all the tender enquiries floated by any central government organisation. It contains all the latest tender released and corrigendums. There’s a special feedback system available to make things more transparent. It will also contain information about the closed tenders.

03. What is expected out of any Government department in this portal?

All users of Government Departments are expected to upload all tender enquiries, its corrigendums and award details after the bid award on to the site.

04.What are all the requirements to use CPP portal?

For a user who has to upload tender enquiries to the portal, the basic requirements would be to get a Login ID created by filling up the registration form.

05. How and where do I get Help to use this portal?

First level help can be obtained by calling the Help line number available on the website. Mails can be sent to the specified mail address.

06. Who is a Nodal Officer?

Nodal officer is the key official who drives the implementation of the project in their Ministry / Department / Organization. He also creates users for his organization.

07. Where do I find training materials / user manuals?

All User Manuals and training materials is available in the Home Page of the portal under the Sub Heading “Help”.

08.What if something goes wrong with password or if I forget it?

There will be alternative option like “forgot password” when you try to login. In case, still if something goes wrong you can connect with us using the helpline numbers.

09. How do I contact my Nodal Officer for any doubts?

The names and contact details of all Nodal Officers are provided in the home page of our portal. So you can easily contact them by getting the details.
