This portal provides a common platform for all bidders to go through the tenders and apply accordingly. There’s a single point access to the procurements made across various central government organisations. All the tenders proposed by various departments are displayed here. Its an application to obtain real time info/track the status of any Government department related tenders by the vendors. It also provides vendors a feedback system, raise their concerns and improve transparency. It combines all the various types of tenders and makes it more easy for bidders to select tenders.
